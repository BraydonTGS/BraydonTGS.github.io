About Me

After spending over 10 years working in some of the best restaurants in Charleston, South Carolina my wife and I decided to drop everything and move to Paris, France. I had a once in a lifetime opportunity to run the kitchen of a small French Bistro in the heart of Paris under the guidance of Chef Daniel Rose. This further deepened my love for cooking, eating and traveling. Along the way we documented our travels with photos, videos, and a blog. We now reside in Birmingham, Alabama where I am pursuing a new career in Software Development. I have spent the past 9 months learning how to program and recently completed a Full-Stack Software Engineering Course with TrueCoders. I love Back-End development, Solving Problems, Working with a Team, and learning about new Technologies.
